Mx A unified mixer, patching matrix and timeline editor


an Mx can host units in it.  units are any type of object such as player, instr, splines, patterns, proxy space vars, ixiquark things, any weird little devices you make, audiofiles (play/record), midi, osc, api.


units have inlets and outlets and you can patch units together with cables.  inlets can go to multiple destinations with variable mapping functions.   so you can easily patch splines into the controls for instr or into a pattern.  you can patch OSC inputs into a pattern.  


its designed to play around and discover things.  works on GUI or by coding.


you can record into a spline in real time.  this is like recording automation on normal DAWs, but since its a spline the controls are continous, not individiual midi-like events.


you can also make one unit record the output of other ones.  you can also put sc code into a unit and do any kind of routine, loops, do things like repatch or send messages to other units.  you can put some sc code in a unit and then drag it on the timeline to where you want it to happen.


time view, zooming, relocating.
